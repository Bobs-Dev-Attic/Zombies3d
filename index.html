Desktop: click to lock the mouse and aim. Phone: left stick moves, right side looks.

How to Play

  • Look & Aim — move the mouse (click the game to lock the pointer), or drag the right half of the screen on touch.
  • Move — WASD / arrows, or the left virtual stick.
  • Sprint — hold Shift (or the » button); drains stamina. Low stamina slows you.
  • Fire — hold Left-click / Space, or the FIRE button. Guns fire where you look; auto weapons hold to spray.
  • Reload — R or ⟳. Swap weapon — Q / mouse-wheel / ↔, or number keys 1–9.
  • Zombie variety — shambling walkers, sprinting runners, hulking brutes that shrug off hits, and spitters that lob acid from range.
  • Headshots — aim high; a shot to the skull drops most zombies instantly.
  • Survive every wave, grab weapon & ammo pickups, then reach the glowing EXIT to advance to the next setting.
